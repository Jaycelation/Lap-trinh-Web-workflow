Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

A quick, classic Italian pasta with garlic, olive oil, and a hint of heat—perfect for weeknights.

Ingredients

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 4 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • Optional: lemon zest and grated Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of well-salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti until 1 minute shy of al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, warm olive oil in a large pan over medium-low heat. Add garlic and cook gently until just golden and fragrant.
  3. Stir in red pepper flakes for 10–20 seconds. Remove from heat if garlic darkens to prevent bitterness.
  4. Reserve about 1 cup of pasta water, then transfer drained spaghetti to the pan.
  5. Over medium heat, toss vigorously with splashes of pasta water to create a glossy emulsion, 1–2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Finish with parsley and optional lemon zest. Serve immediately with grated Parmesan if desired.
